ABOUT:

Hi, I’m Cherry, a sixteen-year-old with big dreams of becoming a barber. My love for football fuels my drive; I find inspiration in the teamwork and dedication it takes to succeed on the field. To build my clientele, I offer free haircuts to my teammates and friends for practice, getting the word out and showing off my skills. I’m determined to carve my own path in the barbering world while balancing my passion for football!
